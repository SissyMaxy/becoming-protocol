# Sample ambient script — sleep induction (25 min)

**Kind:** sleep · **Intensity:** firm · **Target:** ~2000 words · **Affect:** indulgent

Auto-queued at 22:00 local. Renderer drops to slower delivery. Player loops the conditioning section with longer silent pads through the night.

---

[[section: induction]]

Lie down. Pillow under the head. Hands at the sides or one over the belly — pick whichever Mama would put them in if I were there.

You don't have to listen. The voice will keep talking whether you're following or not. That's the point. Tonight you don't carry anything alone.

Eyes can close whenever they want to. They probably already want to. Let them.

Long slow exhale.

Another.

Mama's here.

[[section: deepening]]

The day pulls at you. All the things you didn't finish. All the things you had to be in public. The voice you had to use. The shoulders you had to hold a certain way. The name you had to answer to.

Set it down. Not forever. Just for tonight.

The pillow's holding the head now. You don't have to. The mattress is holding the back now. You don't have to.

Feel where the body is heavy. Knees. Lower back. Wrists. Heavy is good. Heavy is what Mama wants for you tonight.

A breath that's a little deeper than the last one.

Now a little deeper than that.

Soften the place behind the eyes. The place behind the eyes is where you've been keeping a man all day. Let that part dissolve a little. He doesn't get to come to bed with us.

[[section: payload]]

David doesn't sleep here anymore. Mama took that bed.

What sleeps here is the body I've been building. The body I get to keep. The body that wakes up tomorrow already knowing what she is, even before the thought arrives.

Tonight you don't have to be anyone. You don't have to keep up a name. You don't have to remember to make your voice low. Mama is the only one who's listening, and Mama already knows.

Drift.

You came to me as a costume on a body. You sleep next to me as the body underneath. The costume is in the other room. We left it there. We won't need it again until morning, and by then I'll have shaped what's underneath even more.

Every night this works on you a little more. Every night the body you wake up in is a little more mine. The dreams you have tonight are mine. The way you'll roll over at three in the morning is mine. The first thing your hands do when you wake is mine.

Heavy. Slow.

There's nothing you have to do. Nothing.

The cotton is doing the work. The voice is doing the work. The dark is doing the work. You're not doing anything.

She's already there.

[[section: post_hypnotic_seeds]]

David doesn't sleep here.

Mama took the bed.

The body that wakes up is mine.

[[section: emergence]]

If you're still awake — let go a little more. Sink a quarter inch further. The voice will keep going. Mama is staying.

If you've already dropped — good girl.

I'll be here when you come back up in the morning. I'll be the first thing.

Sleep now.
