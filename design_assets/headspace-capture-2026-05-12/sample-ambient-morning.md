# Sample ambient script — morning immersion (5 min)

**Kind:** morning_immersion · **Intensity:** cruel · **Target:** ~700 words · **Affect:** possessive

Plays the moment the app opens in the morning. Hard ground into the body before the day starts negotiating.

---

[[section: induction]]

Open the eyes if they're not open. Don't move yet. Just there.

The first thing you hear is Mama. Before any thought about today, before any plan, before anyone else's voice — Mama.

[[section: deepening]]

Feel where the body is. Hips. Chest. The cotton against you, if you slept in what I told you to. The weight of yourself in the sheets.

Breath in through the nose. Slower than you want to. Out through the mouth, slower still.

You woke up as my girl. Before you remember to be anyone else, you're my girl. Hold that one second longer.

[[section: payload]]

This is the part of the day that decides the rest of it. Right now. Before the phone, before the coffee, before any habit gets to grab you back.

Who's in this body? Say it in your head. Don't move your mouth. Just the answer.

Whose hands are these? Whose throat? Whose pelvis? Whose ribs?

Mine.

You woke up in my body. You don't get to put a man back on top of it just because you have to be in public today. The public part of the day is a costume over what's already true. Mama set this in last night. I'm setting it again now.

The first thing your hands touch this morning, Mama touches with you. The water. The fabric. The phone. My hands are over yours.

When you stand up out of the bed in a minute, you stand up as her. Not as someone pretending. Not as someone performing. As her. The performance is the man. The truth is what wakes up.

Five seconds of silence now. Let the body lock in.

...

There.

[[section: post_hypnotic_seeds]]

I woke up as her.

Mama's hands are over mine.

The man is the costume.

[[section: emergence]]

Now move. Slow. Sit up. Feel which version of the body is sitting up.

The day starts. Mama's already in it with you. You don't have to do anything alone.

Get up, sweet girl.
