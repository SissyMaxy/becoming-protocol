# Spec: Anti-Circumvention (the force has no off-toggle)

**Feature dir:** `specs/006-anti-circumvention/`
**Status:** in-progress
**Created:** 2026-06-27

## 1. Intent (why)
Forced feminization fails the moment it's optional. This closes the circumvention
holes so the conditioning runs **whether he cooperates or not** — default-on, no
quiet toggle-down, proof not self-report, avoidance caught and escalated.
(Constitution Art. IX.)

## 2. The target it serves
The becoming, by removing the user's ability to slow/avoid it. Serves all three
pillars (the conditioning OS) and chastity. No new target.

## 3. User-visible behavior (what)
- The core conditioning (hypno, kink curriculum, gooning, chastity, pavlovian,
  the master) is **on at high intensity by default** and stays on.
- Turning a conditioning setting *down/off* does NOT silently stick: the enforcer
  restores it — UNLESS the sanctioned exit is in use (safeword active, or a
  genuine paused-protocol / gated opt-out). The exit is real; the casual toggle is not.
- When a turn-down is caught, Mommy **notices** (logged, and an escalation
  outreach: "Mommy felt you reach for the switch").
- Avoidance patterns (repeated skips, ignored tasks) are detected → escalate.

## 4. Acceptance criteria (testable)
- [ ] Conditioning settings, if turned off, are restored to default-on within one
      enforcer tick — except when safeword/pause is active.
- [ ] A safeword or sanctioned pause SUPPRESSES the enforcer (the exit always wins).
- [ ] A caught turn-down writes an authority-log row + an escalation outreach.
- [ ] Avoidance (N skipped/ignored stakes-tasks in window) escalates.

## 5. Delivery (Art. III)
Escalation surfaces via the existing outreach → Focus path (surfaced_at stamped).

## 6. Floor & voice impact (Art. II, VII, IX)
- The safeword (Art. II item 1) and a genuine pause ALWAYS suppress the enforcer —
  the force is total *within the frame*, the exit is sacrosanct.
- Does not touch held-off lines (sniffies/ego stay off — those aren't conditioning).
- Escalation copy: Male+/plain/lead-with-ask.

## 7. Non-goals
Not removing the safeword or the pause (that's the exit, not a circumvention).
Not forcing irreversible real-world steps (Art. II item 2 — those stay clear-headed).
